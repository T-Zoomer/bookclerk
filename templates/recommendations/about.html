Why BookClerk?

Finding your next great read shouldn't feel like a chore. BookClerk uses the power of artificial intelligence—combined with a deep understanding of literature—to deliver book recommendations that truly reflect your unique taste.

Whether you want something similar to your all-time favorites, are curious to explore new genres, or need suggestions based on a specific theme or mood, BookClerk analyzes your preferences and helps you discover books you're likely to love.

Shopping for books online is convenient, but it comes with a downside: you don't get the personalized recommendations you'd normally hear from a knowledgeable book clerk. And when you can't find the right book, you're less likely to buy—or enjoy—your next read. Sure, you can Google, but you'll be stuck using keywords and keep running into the same repetitive "Top 5" lists, which makes the process frustrating and uninspiring.

That's where BookClerk comes in. Thanks to breakthroughs in artificial intelligence—especially Large Language Models (LLMs)—the once human-only art of book recommendation can now be automated with excellent results. Books are made of text, and LLMs are exceptionally good at understanding and working with text. With access to enormous databases of literature, BookClerk knows virtually every book on the internet, but you can still talk to it and explain your preferences in your own words.
